InsDown Instructions

  • 1. Copy link in instagram
  • 2. Open InsDown app
  • 3. Allow pasting
  • 4. Wait for the video to download
  • 5. Agree to save to album
  • ps: To download videos for the first time, you need to log in to your account on the app webpage, otherwise you will not be able to download videos.
